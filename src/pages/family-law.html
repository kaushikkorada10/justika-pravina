Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a divorce take?

Mutual consent matters can close within months. Contested cases vary by complexity and court schedule.

Can we change a custody order later?

Yes, if circumstances change. Courts prioritize the child’s best interests and practical parenting plans.

Do I need a prenuptial agreement?

Not mandatory, but useful where assets, business interests, or inheritance considerations are involved.
