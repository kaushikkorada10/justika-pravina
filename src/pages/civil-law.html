Frequently Asked Questions

How long do civil suits take?

Timelines vary by forum and complexity. We pursue interim reliefs and settlement where feasible.

Will I need to attend every hearing?

Not always. Many steps are handled by counsel. Your presence is needed for key stages or evidence.

Can a consumer case run alongside a civil suit?

Yes, where remedies differ. We choose the forum(s) that best protect your interests.
